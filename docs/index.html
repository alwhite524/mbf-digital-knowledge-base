Stewart Park Results

What Changed?

A clearer view of what Beaumont residents received from the project—and where the evidence can be checked.

Improvements

  • Modern playground and recreation facilities
  • Improved walking paths and gathering spaces
  • Expanded opportunities for family and community activities
  • Updated landscaping and park amenities
  • Facilities designed to support community events

Community Impact

The Stewart Park improvements created a more welcoming public space for Beaumont families while adding updated recreational facilities for a growing community.

Why It Matters

The project shows how planning, phased implementation, grants, contracts, and Council actions can combine to produce a long-term public benefit.
