Documented evolution

What Changed?

This section distinguishes changes in the project plan and status from physical improvements that still require completion evidence.

What Changed in the Plan

  • The project evolved into defined Phase I and Phase II packages.
  • Phase I includes trails, pavilions, inclusive playground equipment, splash pad, restrooms, lighting, and parking.
  • Phase II includes a bandshell, secondary playground, skate-park enhancements, basketball relocation, pickleball striping, and picnic shelters.
  • Environmental and grant requirements changed the construction sequence.

What Changed in Status

  • Design and public outreach advanced from 2022 through 2023.
  • Phase I construction was authorized in 2024.
  • Work paused in 2025 to preserve grant eligibility.
  • State clearance was reported in 2026, allowing the project to move forward.

What Is Still Being Verified

Final completion dates, final costs, motions, votes, approved minutes, and video timestamps will be published only after the supporting records are confirmed.
