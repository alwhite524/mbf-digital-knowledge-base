Evidence at a glance

What the record establishes

Operations and Service Model

  • Animal Control operates within the Beaumont Police Department.
  • Field personnel enforce animal-related laws and respond to calls for service within Beaumont.
  • Beaumont historically provided regional Animal Control services through agreements with outside jurisdictions.

Sheltering and Staffing

  • Beaumont uses a contracted sheltering model rather than operating its own full-service municipal animal shelter.
  • Current sheltering services are associated with Haven Pet Center.
  • Available budget records show authorized staffing declined from 3.0 FTE in FY 2018 to 2.0 FTE beginning in FY 2019.
