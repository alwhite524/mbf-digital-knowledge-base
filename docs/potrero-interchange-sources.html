Evidence Standard

How Beaumont Intelligence uses these sources

  • Official project pages are used for current project status but are dated against older records when figures change.
  • Council agenda items establish what was presented and recommended; they are not automatically treated as proof of final action without additional confirmation.
  • Executed agreements, adopted actions, and formal agency records receive greater evidentiary weight.
  • Total project cost, construction investment, and construction-contract award are treated as different financial measures.
  • Historical concept drawings are identified as historical rather than represented as final as-built plans.
  • Where official sources conflict or evolve, the discrepancy is preserved rather than hidden.

Intelligence Assessment

The source register is part of the intelligence

The Potrero Interchange record spans multiple agencies, funding programs, engineering phases, Council decisions, and changing project estimates.

A useful civic-intelligence archive must therefore do more than summarize the project. It must preserve the documents that allow residents to verify how the project changed and why.

This page is intended to serve as that public source register and will continue to expand as new official records are created.
