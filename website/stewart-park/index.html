Project overview

The archive separates confirmed official records from open verification work. Agenda recommendations are not presented as final Council actions unless the official minutes or video have been directly verified.

Verification note: Motions, votes, approved-minutes links, and video timestamps are still being added. The current MVP is source-linked and useful now, but it does not overstate those fields.
